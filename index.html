About Me

I am a back-end web developer with a Master's degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the Federal Technological University of Paraná (UTFPR).

I have experience in full-stack development with a focus on Python-based backend systems, including APIs, data pipelines, and data-driven platforms.

My technical skills include Python, SQL, Django, FastAPI, Docker, AWS, Airflow, and Apache PySpark. I have experience handling high-volume data environments, distributed systems, and web scraping. Currently, I deliver solutions for financial institutions and credit unions, primarily in the areas of credit and risk analysis.

In my master's research, I am involved in predictive modeling and machine learning projects, including public transit demand forecasting and stochastic financial simulation tools.
